### What the gaps look like (LS day-pattern)

Notable pattern: **Koios day 8 of every flagged month is missing from 1PDB across many sites.** Specifically:

* KET 2026-01 day 8, KET 2026-03 day 8 (also days 16 and 24)
* LSB 2026-02, 03, 04 days 8
* MAT 2026-02, 03 day 8 (and others)
* SEH 2026-01 day 8 (and others)
* SHG 2026-02 day 8
* TLH 2026-02 day 8

That clusters around **the 8th and 16th of multiple months**. Likely root cause: the consumption sync timer (every 15 min) only re-pulls a rolling 7-day window, so once a day is missed it never gets re-fetched -- and the original miss correlates with whatever day Koios returned 5xx or the timer was offline. **Prevention** would be a weekly catch-up sweep that ignores the staleness check (`--no-skip`) for the trailing 30-60 days.
